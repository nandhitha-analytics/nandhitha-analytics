## 📂 Projects

### 🔗 Supply Chain Performance Dashboard — Power BI
> *How do you find where a supply chain is bleeding before it shows up in revenue?*

Built an end-to-end interactive Power BI dashboard to monitor supply chain health across warehouses, suppliers, and logistics.

- Designed a **star schema data model** connecting fact and dimension tables across orders, inventory, and delivery data
- Created KPI cards for **Total Orders, Revenue, Gross Margin %, On-Time Delivery %, and Stock on Hand**
- Identified warehouse-level revenue gaps and flagged supplier delay patterns affecting delivery SLAs
- Built dynamic slicers so operations teams could drill into region, product category, and time period without needing to filter in Excel

📌 *Key finding: Supplier delay was the primary driver of missed delivery SLAs — not logistics. The dashboard made that visible for the first time.*

---

### 🔗 HR Analytics & Workforce Intelligence Dashboard — Power BI + Python
> *What does attrition actually look like before someone submits their resignation?*

Analysed workforce data to surface early attrition signals and department-level performance patterns.

- Cleaned and transformed raw HR data using **Python (Pandas)** before loading into Power BI
- Built attrition trend analysis by department, tenure band, and performance rating
- Identified that mid-tenure employees (2–4 years) in specific departments had disproportionately high exit rates
- Designed the report so HR could use it without any technical background — filter-first, insight-led layout

---

### 🔗 Insurance Analytics Dashboard — Power BI + Excel
> *Where is the claims risk actually concentrated?*

Explored insurance policy and claims data to identify high-risk segments and premium performance.

- Modelled relationships between policyholder demographics, claim frequency, and premium adequacy
- Surfaced regional and age-based claim concentration patterns
- Built executive-level summary visuals alongside analyst-level drill-through pages
